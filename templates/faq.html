Do I need an account to use this app?

No–login required. Just pick a deck and start quizzing right away.

Is this app free?

Yes! All features are 100% free and run entirely in your browser.

Which browsers are supported?

Modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari on desktop and mobile.

How do I start a quiz?

Click “Start Quiz” on any deck’s card. You’ll immediately jump into the first question.

How many questions does each quiz have?

By default, we pull in all cards from that deck. You can also select a custom number before starting.

Can I create my own question decks?

Absolutely. On the Dashboard, click “Add Deck,” give it a title, then start adding cards.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays in your browser’s local storage—it never leaves your device.

How do I track my quiz scores?

After each quiz, your score appears on the Dashboard under “Last Studied.”

Can I reset my progress?

Yes. Open any deck, click the gear icon, then choose “Reset Progress.”

My quiz won’t load—what now?

Try clearing your browser cache or reloading the page. If it persists, drop us feedback via the “Help” link.

Questions not displaying correctly?

Make sure your CSV import follows the header question,answer format exactly.
