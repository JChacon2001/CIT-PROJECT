How do I create a new deck?

Click the “Add Deck” button at the top of your Dashboard, give it a name, and click “Save.” Your new deck will appear alongside your others.

Can I rename or delete a deck?

Yes. From the Dashboard, hover over a deck card and click the edit (✎) or delete (🗑) icon that appears.

Where do my decks get stored?

All your decks and cards are stored locally in your browser’s storage and sync to your account when you’re signed in.

How do I back up my decks?

Go to “Unit Library,” click “Export All,” and download a JSON file you can re-import later.

Why aren’t my decks syncing?

Make sure you’re signed in and have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the page if syncing stalls.

How can I add cards to a deck?

Open any deck, click “Add Card,” then fill in “Front” and “Back,” and hit “Save Card.”

Can I reorder cards within a deck?

Yes—drag the handle ☰ next to each card to move it up or down in the list.

What study modes are available?

We offer “Classic” (flash-through), “Quiz” (multiple-choice), and “Spaced Repetition” modes to help reinforce learning.

How do I track my progress?

Each card shows a progress bar. You can also view overall completion on the Dashboard.

The app won’t open on my phone—help!

Try clearing your browser cache or opening in incognito mode. If it still won’t load, let us know via the feedback button.

I lost my account password—what now?

Click “Forgot Password” on the sign-in page. We’ll email you a reset link right away.
