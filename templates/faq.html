Do I need an account to use this app?

No–login required. Just pick a deck and start quizzing right away.

Is this app free?

Yes! All features are 100% free and run entirely in your browser.

Which browsers are supported?

Modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari on desktop.

How do I start a study session?

Click “Study Now!” in the deck page. You’ll immediately jump into the first question.

Can I create my own question decks?

Absolutely. In the "All Decks" section, click “Add Deck,” give it a title, then start adding cards.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays in your browser’s local storage—it never leaves your device.

How do I track my progression?

After each session, your progess appears on a bar in the "All Decks" section, or on the Dashboard page.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes. On the All Decks page, find the deck you wish to reset, then choose “Reset.”

My imports are not displaying correctly.

Make sure your CSV import follows the header question,answer format exactly.
